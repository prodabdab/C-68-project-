clarence public school






Clarence Public School made its humble beginning in the year 1986. Through the years, we have seen a remarkable growth and today the institution is rated as one of the topmost institutions in Bangalore South. The school is located in JP Nagar, Bangalore, the Capital of Karnataka. Bangalore combines a quaint old world charm with the vibrant environment of a Metropolitan City, culturally diverse and historically rich. Bangalore is known for its communal harmony. As is the concern of the parents, the institution emphasizes on two facets of education: Academic Achievement and Discipline. The Institution provides quality education from Pre-Nursery to the Tenth Standard through I.C.S.E stream. Our School environment promotes discipline, independence, interdependence, a love for learning and a sense of social responsibilities. The infrastructure of our school supports excellence in all areas.
